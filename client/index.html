How it works

From stuck to solved
in four steps.

No waitlists. No cold emails. Describe your problem and we will find the right expert, instantly.

Step 01
Describe your doubt

Tell us your domain, skill level, and what you are stuck on. Takes sixty seconds.

Step 02
Get matched instantly

Our algorithm scores available mentors against your profile and surfaces the top three matches.

Step 03
Go live, solve it

Join a private 1-on-1 session with video, whiteboard and chat — all inside one window.

Step 04
Track your growth

Every session is logged. Every doubt solved. Your progress builds into a visible, shareable record.

Platform features

Everything you need.

Draw diagrams, annotate code, explain flows. Both sides collaborate in real time — no third-party tools required.

Verified mentors only
